Tips - Things anyone can do easily

  • Always remember to carry a grocery bag.
  • In summer, buy vegetables from your local farm and help your community.
  • Say NO to food waste and do composting.
  • Stop buying things that are one-time use.
  • Stop buying things impulsively. Have a running list of things you need.
  • Air dry your washed clothes, so that they last longer.
  • Donate your items to Goodwill or other charities.
  • Buy or sell items through Facebook Market Group or Craigslist.
  • Join TerraCycle. You can recycle anything. For individuals, dozens of programs are free.
