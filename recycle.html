What happens to improperly recycled items?

Only 30% of items are recycled. If you don't recycle something properly, it is thrown into a landfill.

How to Recycle Properly

If the cardboard comes in contact with food or liquid, it is not recyclable.

If two or more materials are connected, it is not recyclable unless you take them apart.

Do not tie your recycled items in plastic. Keep them separated.

Plastic containers should be washed and dried. You cannot recycle bendable plastic.
